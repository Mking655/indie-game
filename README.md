# indie-game
this is a colaboration between Royalsoftwareisonline uhohstonky, and potentaily someone else for the 3d/2d talent
Premse:you explore a abandoned ship while you solve time/and space puzzles to escape
Fps, but no movement; you have to use your environment
Objective: without moving, try to escape a room through a door using your environment
Mechanics:
	Time:
		Each item has a certain *time manipulation range, ie: an apple can but turned    rotten by going forward in time, but can't be turned into compost by going super far in time)
		Time effects all objects except you, but you select the object to manipulate
		Before you start, there will be a cutscene of the events that happen in that room that you can manipulate
	Space
		You may rotate and scale objects(to a *limit)
	Movement
		No running, but you may move your self verticy
Elements:
Wood:can be destroyed by plants
Plants: touch wood to rot/ destroy it
Computers: control mechanisms
Humans: login to computers, destroy/trim plants, push buttons
Mechanisms:push player and objects around, crushes everything
Electric wires:interact with computers to turn them on, humans to kill them, fans to turn them on
Weighted objects: fall
Fans: push player and objects around
Doors: always open or can be opened by buttons(doors are the objective)
Buttons:generate electrical current/ open doors
